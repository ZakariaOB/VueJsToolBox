==============================================================================
SUMMARY
==============================================================================
In order to use hygen to generate templating :
  - Generate a generator with action (This is internal and could be : help, new, with-prompt)
     => npx hygen generator new --name 'Name it as you want here'
     => npx hygen generator new --name component

THE IDEA IS THAT HYGEN WILL USE ALL THE TEMPLATES UNDER A GENERATOR AND WILL PLACE THE
PROVIDED ARGUMENTS COMING FROM THE COMMAND LINE TO GENERATE THE RIGHT FILES
CORRECTLY IN THE RIGHT FOLDERS .

     TEST example to generate test-hygen
     => npx hygen component new --name test-hygen --directory test-hygen


- npx here will just execute a command without installing the dependencies internally .
